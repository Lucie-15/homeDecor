Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and home decor advice.

How do I choose the right furniture for my space?
Consider the size of your room, existing decor, functional needs, and your personal style. We offer consultations to help you make the best choices.
Are your products sustainable and ethically sourced?
Yes, we are committed to sustainability. Many of our products are made from recycled materials, and we partner with suppliers who prioritize ethical labor practices.
Do you offer interior design services?
While we don't offer full-scale interior design, our team can provide personalized recommendations and styling tips to help you create your dream space.
What is your return policy for decor items?
We offer a 30-day return policy for most unused and unopened decor items. Please refer to our detailed return policy page for more information.
Can I request custom-made decor items?
Currently, we do not offer custom-made decor items, but we are always expanding our unique collections. Stay tuned for future updates!
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. You can find more details at checkout.
